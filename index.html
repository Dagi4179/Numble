How to Play

Guess the secret 4-digit number in 8 tries. A new puzzle drops every day at 00:00 UTC.
  • 🔢
    Same number worldwide — everyone solves the same puzzle each day.
  • 📍
    Digits can repeat — e.g. 4412 or 9009 are valid targets.
  • 💡
    After each guess you get aggregate clues, not per-digit highlighting.
CLUE TYPES
Green — digit is correct AND in the right position.
Yellow — digit exists but is in the wrong position.
Gray — digit does not appear in the number at all.
