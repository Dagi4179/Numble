How to Play

Guess the secret 4-digit number (all digits unique) in 8 tries. A new puzzle drops every day at 00:00 UTC.
  • 🌍
    Same number worldwide — everyone solves the same puzzle each day.
  • 🔢
    All digits are unique — no repeated digits in the answer ever.
  • 💡
    Each tile reveals its own colour after you submit a guess.
TILE COLOURS
Green — correct digit, correct position.
Yellow — digit exists, wrong position.
Gray — digit not in the number.
EXAMPLE · Secret: 5 8 2 4
5
9
2
8

5 → green (right spot) · 9 → gray (absent) · 2 → yellow (wrong spot) · 8 → yellow (wrong spot)

HINTS (EASY MODE)
Hint 1 — tells you if the answer is Odd or Even.
Hint 2 — reveals the sum of all 4 digits.
